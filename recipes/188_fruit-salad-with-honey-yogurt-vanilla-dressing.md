Fruit Salad with Honey Yogurt Vanilla Dressing
==============================================

**Serves:** 4

 

**Preparation Time:** 30 minutes

**Ingredients:**

-   ¾ cup plain yogurt
-   2 Tablespoons honey
-   1 Tablespoons poppy seeds
-   1 teaspoon vanilla extract
-   1 teaspoon orange zest
-   4 cups assorted fruit of choice (preferably at least one type of citrus)

 

**Instructions:**

Mix together 1st 5 ingredients until honey is dissolved. (it might help to warm the honey slightly so that it will dissolve easier.) Combine with fruit in a bowl. Let chill for 20 minutes. Serve as a side dish, or a dessert!
